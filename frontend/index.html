Pipeline Architecture

1
Tokenize & Clean: Raw strings are stripped of stop-words and punctuation, then lowercased.
2
Expand Query: Maps occasions to semantic tag dictionaries (e.g. "wedding" includes "bride, ceremony").
3
TF-IDF Vectorization: TF counts normalized by length; IDF smoothed using `log(1 + N/(1+DF))`.
4
Cosine Similarity: Inner product divided by Euclidean norms. Direct title matches receive a +15% boost.
